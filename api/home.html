How It Works

Connecting Travelers with Local Hosts

Ohana was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to create a world where anyone can belong anywhere. We believe that people who travel should have the freedom to experience places like locals do, and hosts should have the opportunity to earn income by sharing their spaces.

Today, millions of hosts and travelers choose Ohana to create unforgettable experiences. Whether you're looking for a weekend getaway or planning your next big adventure, our platform connects you with unique accommodations around the globe.

Earn Money Hosting

Share Your Space, Create Connections

Turn your extra space into extra income. Whether you have a spare room, an entire home, or a unique property, Ohana makes it easy to start hosting and welcoming travelers from around the world.

  • Set your own schedule and pricing
  • Get paid securely through our platform
  • Access to millions of potential guests
  • Dedicated support from our host success team
